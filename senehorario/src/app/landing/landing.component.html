Senéhorario Build conflict-free schedules

Plan Your Academic Schedule Like the Pros

A scheduling tool designed for Universidad de los Andes students. Build your ideal timetable by exploring course combinations, avoiding conflicts, and optimizing your semester.

  • Search by NRC, code, or name and inspect sections fast
  • Automatic validation for overlaps and required pairings
  • Compare multiple valid schedules with a weekly calendar view
