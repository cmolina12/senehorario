Senéhorario

Your perfect Uniandes schedule, made simple.

Senéhorario is a web tool for Universidad de los Andes' students designed to help users optimize their academic schedule. Its goal is to make it easy to create the perfect class timetable by balancing preferences like course combinations, free time, and availability.

Senéhorario is an open source project created and maintained by Camilo Molina. The intention is to provide a free and accessible scheduling tool for the Uniandes community, and contributions or feedback are always welcome. You can find the source code and contribute on GitHub:
